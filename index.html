Benefits of hiking with our dogs

Hiking is super fun and it is even more fun when you hike with a dog. They love to pee on everything and get all wet and dirty. They love to motivate you by running ahead and making sure you keep your heart rate up, while getting your steps in. Some of our dogs get tired on the second part of the hike and this can be a good reminder to slow down and not overdo it on the way back.
